Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy aims to describe the methods of managing the processing of personal data of users interacting with our e-commerce website, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR - EU Regulation 2016/679).

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Markommerce, with registered office at Via Cisanello 3, Pisa 56121, Italy. For any request regarding the protection of personal data, you can contact us at: mKo@markommerce.com.

2. Types of Data Collected

During the use of our e-commerce, we may collect different types of personal data, including:

  • Identification data (name, surname, email address, home/shipping address);
  • Contact data (phone number, email address);
  • Payment data (credit card information or other payment methods);
  • Navigation data (IP address, browser type, browsing data);
  • Purchase data (purchased products, completed orders);

3. Purpose of Data Processing

The personal data collected are processed for the following purposes:

  • Order management and fulfillment;
  • Payment and financial transaction processing;
  • Sending communications related to the status of the order;
  • Customer support and problem resolution related to purchases;
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations (e.g., invoicing);
  • Statistical analysis to improve our services and website;
  • Direct marketing (with explicit user consent), including promotional offers and newsletters.

4. Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes listed above, in accordance with applicable regulations. Order-related data will be kept for 10 years for tax and accounting purposes.

5. Data Sharing

Your personal data may be shared with:

  • Payment service providers and financial institutions for managing transactions;
  • Couriers and shipping services for order delivery;
  • Data analytics companies to improve navigation experience and optimize our marketing campaigns;
  • Legal entities or public authorities in compliance with legal obligations.

6. User Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and request a copy of it;
  • Request correction of inaccurate data or the completion of incomplete data;
  • Request the deletion of personal data ("right to be forgotten");
  • Restrict the processing of personal data under the conditions set out in Article 18 of the GDPR;
  • Object to the processing of data for direct marketing purposes;
  • Request data portability to another data controller;
  • Lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority (the Data Protection Authority in your country).

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, destruction, loss, or accidental alteration. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. The changes will be published on this page, so we encourage you to check it periodically.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers.
How can I track my order?
After your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with the tracking information. You can also track the order in your account dashboard.
Can I return or exchange a product?
Yes, you can return or exchange a product within 30 days of purchase, as long as it is in its original condition. Please visit our return policy page for more details.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on the destination.
How long does it take to receive my order?
Delivery times depend on the shipping method chosen at checkout and your location. Typically, domestic orders are delivered within 3-7 business days, while international orders can take up to 14 business days.
What happens if my order is damaged during shipping?
If your order arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at support@example.com with your order number and photos of the damage. We will assist you in resolving the issue by offering a replacement or refund.
Can I change or cancel my order after it has been placed?
Once an order has been placed, we process it quickly. If you need to make changes or cancel it, please contact us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee changes if the order has already been processed.
Do you offer gift cards?
Yes, we offer electronic gift cards in various denominations. These can be purchased on our website and sent to the recipient via email.
Are your products eco-friendly?
We are committed to sustainability. Many of our products are made from eco-friendly materials, and we strive to use minimal packaging. Please check the product description for specific details.
